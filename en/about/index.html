About

I’ve been working in IT since 2016, starting my bachelor’s in Business Informatics at Cooperative State University Baden Wuerttemberg, which I graduated in 2019 with a high enthusiasm for Software Engineering. I was taken by the idea of needing nothing but a notebook and code to create services and products that have financial or social significance or generate attention.

It followed my master’s in Web Engineering, for which I was drawn to University of Applied Sciences Salzburg, Austria. The program provided the perfect framework to expand my expertise in full-stack, continuous development and cloud computing. Shortly after starting my master’s studies, I began to develop software as a freelancer according to customer requirements. This is still the case today, so feel free to contact me if you’re developing software with JavaScript and need reliable and competent support. During the master’s, I also developed Expo Generator with my highly esteemed fellow students - a great experience, to which I dedicated this post.

The master’s thesis was again to take place in Germany, where artiso solutions GmbH welcomed me. My work revolved around the prediction of air quality in office environments with the perspective of enabling intelligent, digital assistance for indoor ventilation. With this work I fulfilled my goal to experience the complete Data Science process in real life. My findings fed into this post.

At the end of 2021, the enthusiasm I developed during my undergraduate studies eventually led to my current employment as Innovation Engineer at artiso. I am very excited to discover innovative products, significantly design them and bring them to life as corporate startups through software engineering.
